Our story

Born in Boston.

Offscript Holdings is a Boston-based media and marketing company. We're the team behind The Boston Calendar, New England's most popular event discovery platform, and Joyraft, our social experience app.

Over a decade of building for Boston's audiences has given us something rare: real, proprietary data on how people discover and engage with local experiences. We know this audience because we built the platform they use every day.

We're digital storytellers and growth marketers. We help organizations across the region reach the right people, at the right moment, with the right story, and we measure everything.

Now we're taking those learnings beyond our own platforms and working with a select group of partners to do for their brands what we've done for Boston.
